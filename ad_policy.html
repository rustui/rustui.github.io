Privacy Policy for TrueSharp

Effective Date: January 31, 2025

Thank you for using TrueSharp! This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the TrueSharp iOS application.

Information Collection and Use

TrueSharp, the application itself, does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about its users. We do not require you to provide any personal data to use the app, and we do not track your activities within the app.

Third-Party Services - AdMob

TrueSharp uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service, to display advertisements within the application. AdMob may collect and use certain information about your device and your interaction with ads. This information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising IDs)
  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address
  • Usage data related to your interaction with ads

The information collected by AdMob is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We strongly encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy to understand how AdMob collects, uses, and shares information. You can find Google's Privacy Policy here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Additionally, here is the link to AdMob specific information within Google's policy:

https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

Your Choices

You can control how Google personalizes ads for you through your Google account settings or by opting out of personalized advertising on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app or through other appropriate means. Your continued use of TrueSharp after any changes to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such changes.
