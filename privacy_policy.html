Welcome to our applications (the "Services"), provided by us ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our Services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We want to be transparent about the data involved when you use our Services.

1. Information We Do Not Collect Directly

Our Services are designed to respect your privacy. We do not directly collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from you through the Services themselves. This means we do not collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, physical address, photos, or contacts directly.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

While we do not collect data directly, our Services use Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google, to display advertisements. AdMob helps us keep our Services available, often free of charge.

AdMob may collect and use certain data to provide personalized advertising and measure ad performance. This data may include, but is not limited to:

  • Device Information: Such as your device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (like the Advertising Identifier - IDFA on iOS), and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the ads shown in the Services (e.g., clicks, views, time spent).
  • Location Information: General location information (e.g., country or city) may be inferred from your IP address or provided if location services are enabled for advertising purposes on your device.
  • IP Address: Your Internet Protocol address.

This data is collected and processed by Google AdMob according to their own privacy policies. We do not have direct access to or control over the specific data collected by AdMob or how they use it.

We encourage you to review Google's privacy policies to understand how they handle your data:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Ad Technology Information: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

3. How Information Collected by AdMob is Used

The data collected by Google AdMob is primarily used to:

  • Serve relevant and personalized advertisements to you within the Services.
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.
  • Improve AdMob's services.
  • Detect and prevent fraud and ensure security.

4. Your Choices Regarding Advertising

You may have options to limit personalized advertising on your iOS device:

  • App Tracking Transparency (ATT): On iOS 14.5 and later, apps are required to ask for your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising purposes. You can manage these permissions in your device's Settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking.
  • Limit Ad Tracking (Older iOS versions) / Personalized Ads (Newer iOS versions): You may be able to opt-out of interest-based advertising through your device settings. Check under Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads (turn off). Instructions may vary slightly depending on your iOS version.

Please note that opting out may not stop all advertising, but it may make the ads you see less relevant to your interests.

While we do not collect your personal data directly, we rely on Google AdMob to implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the data they collect. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
