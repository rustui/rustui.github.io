Pop Mania!

Ready for some satisfying destruction?

Welcome to Pop Mania!, the perfect casual game for your downtime! Dive into a world where precision meets demolition.

How to Play

Tap to aim and launch balls downwards, striking intricate objects assembled from geometric shapes. Watch with delight as your shots chip away at the structures, piece by piece. Thanks to our incredibly realistic physics engine, every hit feels impactful!

Land enough hits, and the objects will crumble entirely in a cascade of satisfying destruction!

Master the Physics & Special Items

It's not just about breaking things down. Master the physics to create awesome chain reactions as falling debris takes out other objects below!

Keep an eye out for special items: rescue trapped balls to add another ball to your arsenal, boosting your popping power.

Just be careful – treacherous black holes can swallow your balls if you're not precise!

Perfect For Your Downtime

Pop Mania! is designed for quick bursts of fun. It's the ideal companion whether you have a few minutes to spare, need a moment to relax, or want an engaging distraction while watching your favorite show.

Download Pop Mania! today and experience the simple joy of popping!
